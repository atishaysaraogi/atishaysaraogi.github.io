Case Manager

Problem: A temporary housing shelter for families handles its cases through pen-and-paper forms which is cumbersome and inefficient to manage.

Project: develop + innovate for social change & Good News Partners

Solution: A free, intuitive web app to manage client information from anywhere and extract metrics as required.

Team: Patrick Hoey, Sarah Fazio, Cheryl Lin, Minseo Kim, Jake Gallardo, Atishay Saraogi

A product that will actually be used

I led my team to interview the client and observe the current functioning of the shelter to understand the constraints better. We learnt that it must be free and intuitive for it to be actually used. It must be simple enough to replace pen and paper and be passed on to the next director of the shelter.

  • Access control to allow other employees to use the information management system
  • Metrics extracted from data, as required to fill out state and federal forms
  • An instruction manual for users and documentation for future development
